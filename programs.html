Our Programs

Discover our comprehensive educational programs designed to inspire and empower students

Physics Program

Master the fundamentals of physics through hands-on learning

Our physics program is designed to help students develop a strong foundation in physics concepts through interactive lessons and real-world applications. We focus on making complex topics accessible and engaging.

What We Offer:

  • Interactive physics lessons
  • Hands-on experiments
  • Real-world applications
  • AP Physics preparation

Program Levels:

  • Beginner: Basic physics concepts and principles
  • Intermediate: Advanced topics and problem-solving
  • Advanced: AP Physics preparation and college-level concepts

Grade Levels: Incoming 8th–9th graders, AP Physics 1 for incoming 11th–12th graders

Class Format: Weekly lessons, hands-on experiments, and problem-solving sessions

Chess Program

Develop strategic thinking through chess

Our chess program is designed to develop strategic thinking, problem-solving skills, and mental discipline. Students learn through a combination of theory and practice, with instruction ranging from basic to advanced levels.

What We Offer:

  • Basic to advanced chess strategies
  • Regular practice sessions
  • Tournament preparation
  • One-on-one coaching

Program Levels:

  • Beginner: Learn the basics and fundamental strategies
  • Intermediate: Develop tactical skills and opening theory
  • Advanced: Master complex strategies and tournament play

Grade Levels: All skill levels welcome

Class Format: Weekly lessons, practice sessions, and tournament preparation
