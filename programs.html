Chess Program

Develop strategic thinking through chess

Our chess program is designed to develop strategic thinking, problem-solving skills, and mental discipline. Students learn through a combination of theory and practice, with instruction ranging from basic to advanced levels.

What We Offer:

  • Basic to advanced chess strategies
  • Regular practice sessions
  • Tournament preparation
  • One-on-one coaching

Program Levels:

  • Beginner: Learn the basics and fundamental strategies
  • Intermediate: Develop tactical skills and opening theory
  • Advanced: Master complex strategies and tournament play

Grade Levels: All skill levels welcome

Class Format: Weekly lessons, practice sessions, and tournament preparation
